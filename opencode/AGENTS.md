1. **Branch names**: Use descriptive names (e.g., `feature/agent-improvements`, `bugfix/agent-error-handling`)
2. **Commit messages**: Clear and concise, explain the "why" not just the "what"
3. **Make small commits**: Split changes into multiple small commits when possible for easier review
